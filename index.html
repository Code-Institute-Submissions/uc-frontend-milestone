About

Counterpoint Fitness is a one-man operation - always has been and always will be. My name is Alastair. I'm originally from Bristol in England, but moved to Ireland in 2016. I'm married to a lovely Irish lady.

I set up Counterpoint Fitness at the start of 2017 with the goal of helping people that live and work in Blackrock get great results - I want to help people who don't like gyms but want to be fitter, stronger and enjoy better energy and vitality.

I lost my brother in 2010 when I was just entering my final year of university studying music technology. One thing that kept me going over a difficult two years was weight training, exercise and good nutrition. I learned first hand the value that staying on top of your physical health has for your mental health.

I wanted to help others experience the great things that exercise and nutrition can do for all areas of life. So I studied personal training, got qualified and started working as a PT in 2012 in Bristol. I love seeing people I train get healthier, happier and more confident.
